# SecureWealth Twin — Demo Script (5 Minutes)

## 0. Preparation
1. Ensure backend is running: `uvicorn backend.main:app --reload`
2. Ensure frontend is running: `expo start`
3. Have 3 terminal tabs ready for persona resets if needed.

## 1. Onboarding & First Impressions (1 Min)
- **Action:** Open the app, show the Welcome screen.
- **Narrative:** "Meet SecureWealth Twin, your AI-powered financial guardian. We're not just tracking expenses; we're building a digital twin of your financial future."
- **Action:** Tap "Get Started", show the Login/Persona selection.
- **Narrative:** "We'll start with Ananya, a first-jobber. Her dashboard is clean because she's just starting. Notice the 'Build Emergency Fund' action — our Strategy Engine identifies this as her #1 priority."

## 2. The Command Center (1 Min)
- **Action:** Switch to Priya (Software Engineer).
- **Narrative:** "Now let's look at Priya. She has a complex portfolio. Her Net Worth is front and center, but look at the 'Portfolio Health' score. It's not just about how much you have, but how well it's working for you."
- **Point out:** "The 'Month-End Pressure' insight. Our Behaviour Engine detected a spending spike in the last 7 days of the month, which could lead to a cash crunch."

## 3. The Power of 'What-If' (1 Min)
- **Action:** Navigate to Goals -> Home Goal -> Run Scenario.
- **Narrative:** "This is the Scenario Simulator. What if Priya cuts her Swiggy spending by 30% and redirects it to her SIP?"
- **Action:** Move the 'Reduce Spend' slider.
- **Narrative:** "Instantly, she sees the 'Cost of Inaction'. By making this small habit change, she hits her Home goal 8 months sooner and adds ₹4.5 Lakhs to her 10-year wealth projection."

## 4. Conversations with Context (1 Min)
- **Action:** Open AI Chat. Ask: "Why is my stress score high?"
- **Narrative:** "Our Chat Advisor isn't generic. It knows Priya's data. It explains her stress score is high due to that month-end spike and lack of a liquid buffer."
- **Point out:** "The responses are concise, actionable, and token-efficient, keeping the app fast and low-cost."

## 5. Security & Portfolio Health (1 Min)
- **Action:** Show Portfolio Health breakdown.
- **Narrative:** "Finally, every action is guarded by our Fraud Hooks. Whether it's adding an asset or changing a goal, we have stubs ready for a deep cybersecurity layer integration, ensuring SecureWealth Twin is as safe as it is smart."

## 6. Closing
- "SecureWealth Twin: Smart. Safe. Your financial future, decoded. Thank you!"
